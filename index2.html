Buddeez was created to provide young adults between the ages of 13 - 24 a community where they can help each other grow and build relationships with others who are going through similar situations. We have observed that resources aren’t as available as we we think they are. We wanted Buddeez so that anyone who struggles with depression or anxiety or could use a mental health recharge in finding a place where you can speak with a mentor or even connect with a mentee for a better tomorrow.

Everyone has potential, by partnering with Buddeez and supporting our mentoring program, you can help better understand and help these young individuals. At Buddeez, we help you see the biggest possible future for the youth facing mental and depression that can often hold one back from seeing what matters most; themselves. Our mentor program supports our community, resulting in a positive outcome for everyone. Studies have shown that mentored youth have a better outlook on life as well as their social surroundings, ultimately helping by building up confidence.
